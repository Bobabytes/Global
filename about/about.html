Hi! I'm me.

▹ Code connoisseur, web wizard, and boundary-breaking computer science maverick; I'm fusing artistic flair with technological mastery to revolutionize the digital realm.

▹ This is a website I've made in the span of two weeks for my Web Programming course. I hope my Professor is impressed! He deserves no less in terms of effort.

▹ Some developer commentary:
The HTML was by far the most time-consuming. Figuring out what I liked and making a cohesive(I hope) theme was more difficult than any JS implementation - and throughout these two weeks, I taught the principles of flexboxes to myself so I can make it all dynamic. Experimenting with CSS styles is a bottomless pit that I could spend weeks upon weeks playing around with, but I really like this sleek card theme; one that I learned from our Wizarding Quiz example. The images you see throughout this website have been compiled, recolored and edited by me via Photoshop, although the source material for all the assets on this website were found on Freepik and PngEgg, while the fonts were found on Adobe Fonts and Google Fonts.
